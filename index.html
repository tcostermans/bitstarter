Locator will make it able to retrieve your stolen or misplaced items. You can label your favorite items like a phone or a bike with a QR code sticker. You can manage all your items online. If you misplace your phone or it gets stolen, you just need to flag your device as missing and locator will do the rest. If someone finds the device, the QR code can be scanned using the android or IOS application. You will get notified that your phone was found. Through the user friendly online interface the person that found your device will have several options to get in touch with you. The end-user has control over his personal data, he/she controls what personal information (e-mail,cellphone number,..) he wants to share with the person that found the device. For security minded persons an encrypted online messaging system will be available.

Locator is a subscription based service. For 30 EUR a year you can register up to 5 devices. After signing up, you will receive a Locator quick start package containing 5 QR code stickers to label your phone, bike, laptop, ... Using our website or mobile appication, your will be able to manage your devices and flag them as missing. Based on your subscription level (bronze,silver,gold) you will get more info about your missing device once it gets scanned. Gold customers will get a GPS location of where there missing device was scanned. Based on your additional membership level you will have a lot of options(e-mail,Twitter,Facebook,cell phone,...) to get into touch with the person that located your device. If you want, you can promote a finders fee. If someone finds your phone and uses the locator mobile app(android or IOS), he/she will be informed there is a finders fee by the mobile application.He/she will also have the option to mark the phone as found.

FAQ

Can I register more than 5 devices per Locator account?
Registering more than 5 devices is possible, you can add to 100 items per Locator account. Purchasing additional devices will come at a small fee.
Will Locator work in my country?
Locator only requires an internet connection and should therefor work worldwide. In the startup fase Locator will be only available in the EU, but we plan on making Locator available for the rest of the world soon after hitting the EU market.
What about privacy?
Locater respects your privacy! You are in control of your own personal information. You control which data you want to share. Scanning a QR code of a device that is not flagged as missing won't display any data of the owner of a device.
